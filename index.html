Objective

Our senior project involves the development of a CubeSat, a miniature satellite, with a primary focus on enhancing the security of communications between ground control stations and the satellite itself. By implementing advanced encryption techniques, we aim to establish a robust and secure communication channel that safeguards critical data transmissions, commands, and telemetry. This project not only advances our understanding of satellite technology but also addresses the pressing need for secure and private communication in space operations.
